TWENDEHAJA USER INSTRUCTIONS

This application contains two parts namely:

View public toilets
View portable toilets

NB: The application requires you to turn on your data connection and gps

View Public Toilets

A map loads with markers of public toilets nearby. It then populates the first five nearest places in a list and calculates and displays their distance from your current location.On click of the nearest place it populates a new screen which give you several options to choose from. You can get both driving and walking directions to your destination.

View Portable Toilets

This part allows you to view the profile of the companies who offer portable toilet services. On click one of the profiles it takes you to the the companies portfolio where you are able to view the companies recent work. You can choose to book the company or you can comment on their portfolio
